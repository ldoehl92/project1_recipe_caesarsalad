Caesar Salad

Dressing recipe by Jenn Segal, Once Upen A Chef Website
Makes 2-3 servings

Ingredients

For the dressing:

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp anchovy paste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worchester sauce
  • 6 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper

For the salad*:

  • 12 iceberg leaves
  • 2 pitta breads
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • Herbs: Rosemary, Basil, Thyme
  • Neutral tasting oil for frying
  • 60g Grana Padano

The salad can be as imaginative as you like ;-). The ingredients are suggestions on what fits to the salad from my experience.
Alternatives to the:

  • salad: Romaine salad
  • bread: ciabatta
  • meat: chicken or prawn. Use a chicken quorn for a vegetarian version

Procedure

  1. Mince the garlic.
  2. Whisk the garlic, anchovy paste, lemon juice, mustard and Worchester sauce in a small bowl.
  3. Add the mayonnaise and olive oil to the bowl and whisk all ingredients for the dressing together. Season the dressing with salt and pepper after preference.
  4. Cut the fish and bread to coarse chunks.
  5. Heat the neutral oil in a large frying pan and insert the fish chunks. While frying season the fish with the herbs and pepper. Cook until the fish is cooked on the inside light pink. Finally, remove the fish into a large (glass) bowl and set aside.
  6. Reheat oil in the pan and insert the bread chunks. Fry the bread until it browns and becomes lightly crispy. Remove the bread to the cooked salmon.
  7. Peel the leaves from the salad head. Break the leaves to smaller pieces. Wash and rinse them, and then add them to the salmon and bread.
  8. Grate the cheese and add it to the salad mixture as well.
  9. Stir in the dressing and mix everything well.
  10. Enjoy your meal! Guten appetit!
